Main Destination Information

  • Bora Bora is located in the South Pacific Ocean, part of French Polynesia.
  • Approximately 29.3 square kilometers (11.3 square miles) in area.
  • Around 10,000 inhabitants.
  • French and Tahitian are the official languages.
  • Tropical climate with a wet season from December to March and a dry season from April to November.
  • Currency is French Pacific Franc (XPF).
  • Key attractions include stunning lagoons, coral reefs, Mount Otemanu, and luxury resorts.
  • Activities include snorkeling, scuba diving, beach relaxation, and boat tours.
  • Accessible by air from Tahiti, with limited international flights.
  • A popular honeymoon destination known for its beauty and luxury accommodations.
  • Local cuisine features fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and traditional Polynesian dishes.
  • The island is famous for its overwater bungalows and vibrant coral gardens.
  • Bora Bora hosts several cultural festivals, showcasing traditional music and dance.
  • Best visited between April and October for optimal weather conditions.
